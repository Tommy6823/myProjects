Ťuk ťuk... kto je?
...hlúpe srdce moje.

love of the stag

Held on the edge of a cliff you jump,
with worm embrace from your holder.
You smile, as you revel in the beauty of you Becoming.
You needed him, you felt needed.
Willing to lay down your life you fight for yours and his.
You breathe in the passionate act of embrace and self-preservation,
the knife dripping of your blood used as a tool of revelation.

It is beautiful.

Now your story is complete.
You are ready. You understand. You love…

“In the end, it is impossible not to become what others believe you are.”
